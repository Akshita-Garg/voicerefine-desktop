How it works

Speak. Refine. Paste.

Three steps from thought to typed text, entirely on your machine.

1
Press the hotkey
Hit Ctrl+Shift+Space (or your custom shortcut) anywhere — the recording overlay appears without stealing focus from your app.
2
Speak naturally
Say whatever you want to type. Press the hotkey again to stop. The overlay transcribes your audio using a local model — no internet needed.
3
Text appears
Your words, cleaned up and refined, are pasted directly into the focused window — whether it's Slack, Notion, email, or a code editor. Every dictation is also copied to your clipboard, so Ctrl+V always works as a fallback.
4
Review & re-refine
Open the main window for longer dictation sessions, compare raw vs. refined output, and tweak refinement settings to match your style.
Modes

Tuned for speed
and accuracy.

Pick the transcription quality and refinement style that fits what you're writing.

Transcription
Quick
Whisper Tiny
Fastest response. Good for short notes where you want zero lag. Runs fully on-device.
Balanced
Parakeet 0.6B
Default. A warm local server cuts latency on repeated use. Strong accuracy for everyday dictation.
Precise
Cohere Transcribe
Highest accuracy, especially for technical or uncommon words. Downloaded on first use (~1.5 GB).
Refinement
Clean
Clean
No LLM. Strips filler words, fixes punctuation, and removes speech artifacts instantly — zero latency.
Transform
Smart Format
Keeps your voice and vocabulary. Cleans structure, handles spoken lists and corrections. Conservative rewrite.
Transform
Polish & Organize
Lightly rewrites for flow and clarity, merges repetition, restructures when you signal a list. Great for longer dictation.
Features

Built for daily use.

Everything you need to make dictation a real part of your workflow.

🎙️
Global Hotkey Overlay
Record and paste into any app without switching windows. The overlay stays out of your way and cleans up after itself.
🤖
Built-in Local LLM
Gemma 3 1B runs entirely on your machine for Transform refinement. GPU-accelerated when available, CPU fallback always works.
☁️
Optional Cloud Refinement
Prefer Gemini or GPT-4o mini? Add your API key in Settings. If selected, refinement text is sent directly to that provider, never to VoiceRefine servers.
⚙️
Customizable Prompts
Edit the refinement prompt for each preset directly in Settings. Tune the exact output style to match how you write.
🔥
Warm Model Loading
The selected transcription model is kept warm, and Transform loads the local LLM only when you choose model-powered refinement.
🛠️
Main Window for Review
Long dictation sessions, side-by-side raw vs. refined output, and full settings — all in one clean window.
